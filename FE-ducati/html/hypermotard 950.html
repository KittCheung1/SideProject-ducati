Hypermotard 950

Play time. Show time.

Challenge accepted. For the 2022 range, available from June 2021, Ducati has updated the Hypermotard 950 family to Euro 5 regulation standards* and introduces the new livery for the SP version. The update guarantees the same values in terms of torque and output. A new colour scheme that evokes the racing world with its colours and that of freestyle sports with its graphics, highlighting the youthful character of the bike. The ideal choice for those looking to indulge in the pure pleasure of an adrenaline rush while riding their bike, having maximum fun without compromising on safety. The three models - Hypermotard 950, Hypermotard 950 RVE and Hypermotard 950 SP - are perfect for taking cornering to extremes whilst also dealing with city traffic with the utmost agility.
